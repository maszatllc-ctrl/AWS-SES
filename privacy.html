Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2025  |  Last Updated: January 1, 2025

QuoteBoosters ("we," "us," or "our") operates the website quoteboosters.com and the daily email newsletter service. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information when you use our Service.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum information necessary to provide our service:

  • Email Address: Collected when you voluntarily subscribe to our daily quote newsletter.
  • Name (optional): If you choose to provide your first name for personalized greetings.
  • Usage Data: We may collect non-personally identifiable information such as email open rates and click-through data to improve our service. This data is aggregated and cannot be used to identify individual users.
  • Device & Browser Information: Standard log data such as IP address, browser type, and operating system, collected automatically when you visit our website.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely for the following purposes:

  • To deliver our daily motivational quote newsletter to your inbox.
  • To send transactional emails, such as subscription confirmations and unsubscribe confirmations.
  • To respond to your inquiries or support requests.
  • To analyze aggregated usage patterns to improve our content and service quality.

We do not use your email address for unsolicited marketing, and we will never send you emails unrelated to the QuoteBoosters service without your explicit consent.

3. Consent and Opt-In

We operate on a strict opt-in basis. You will only receive emails from us after you have voluntarily provided your email address and confirmed your subscription through our double opt-in process. You may withdraw your consent and unsubscribe at any time (see Section 6).

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. We may share data only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: We use trusted third-party services (such as Amazon Web Services for email delivery) to operate our service. These providers are contractually obligated to protect your data and use it only for providing services to us.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or governmental authority.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information, including encryption in transit (TLS/SSL), secure storage, and access controls. While no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure, we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your data.

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Unsubscribe: Every email we send includes a one-click unsubscribe link at the bottom. You can opt out at any time, and we will process your request immediately.
  • Data Access: You may request a copy of the personal data we hold about you by emailing us at privacy@quoteboosters.com.
  • Data Deletion: You may request that we permanently delete all personal data associated with your account by emailing us. We will fulfill deletion requests within 30 days.
  • Data Correction: You may request corrections to any inaccurate personal data.

7. Data Retention

We retain your email address and subscription data for as long as you maintain an active subscription. If you unsubscribe, we will delete your personal data within 30 days, except where we are required by law to retain certain records.

8. Cookies

Our website uses only essential cookies necessary for the basic functionality of the site. We do not use tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or third-party analytics cookies that track individual users.

9. Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal data, we will take steps to delete that information promptly.

10. International Data Transfers

If you are accessing our service from outside the United States, please be aware that your data may be transferred to and processed in the United States where our servers are located. By subscribing, you consent to this transfer.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of our service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

12. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to request disclosure of what personal information we collect, request deletion of your data, and opt out of the sale of personal information. We do not sell your personal information. To exercise your rights, contact us at privacy@quoteboosters.com.

13. GDPR Compliance

For users in the European Economic Area, we process data based on your consent (opt-in subscription). You have the rights to access, rectify, erase, restrict processing, and port your data. To exercise these rights, contact privacy@quoteboosters.com.

14. CAN-SPAM Compliance

We fully comply with the CAN-SPAM Act. All of our emails include our physical mailing address, clear identification of the message as an advertisement (where applicable), honest subject lines, and a functioning one-click unsubscribe mechanism.
